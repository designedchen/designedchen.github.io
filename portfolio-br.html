How can we improve Baskin Robbin's app?

Who is Baskin-Robbins?

BASKIN ROBBINS IS KNOWN AS A TOP AMERICAN
CHAIN ICE CREAM AND CAKE SPECIALITY SHOP.

Baskin Robbins was founded in 1945 and started off with the idea of
offering 31 flavors of ice cream. It claims to be world’s largest chain of ice
cream speciality stores. Today, the company has introduced 1300+ flavors.
Their mobile presence is currently disengaging the user’s experience due
to an unrecognizable user interface, and a frustrating journey while using the app.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

I am doing a quick design challenge on redesiging the app. When I first start using this app, the overall experience was not good because there are elements that are unexpected and unpredictable. **Side note: I do not work for Baskin Robbins, and this case study are based on my ideas and opinions. I do not have any access to Baskin Robbins' research and data.

Project Scope

Problems:
I discover there are a lot of problems that made this product a failure. One of the possibility of this product fails is because lack of product vision and consumer's need and goals.

Pain point:
After using the app, I found few problems:
  • Home page is full of advertising feeling.
  • Few of the UIs are confusing.
  • Links did not take me to the page that I assume it should be.
  • Easy to get lost and had to question myself how did I get to this page.

Project Goals:
To redesign the overall experience of the Baskin Robbin apps to retain customers and bring a valuable experience.

Tools Used:
Sketch, Invision, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator

Roles Played:
Redesign the user experience based on research to conception and visualization and testing.

Project Process

Research


I will be conducting a heuristic evaluation to identify usability problems in the user interface and user experience design. I will be testing the app multiple time and observe carefully to make sure I catch all the problems.

Evaluate the home page

Evaluate the menu links

Evaluate the User Interface

Evaluate the store location

Research: Competitor's App


I will be conducting and testing competitor's app based of three category: Aesthetic, User Interface, and Ease of use navigating. The rating is based on 1-10 scale for 1 being the worst and 10 being the best. The result of the test clearly shows that some competitor apps are doing better or worse than Baskin Robbins. Those that did well have similar design pattern where it consists of 4-5 icon at the bottom of the screen. Majority of the apps have its own UI elements and they all are comprehensive.

Research: Understanding the current app site map


I doing a sitemap study trying to understand the current design and see spot any problems that I am not aware. After drawing the sitemap, it is clearly that I spot problems on the sitemap. There are multiple primary links that will link to same secondary page which is why it confuses the user. It definitely makes the user fustrated easily without any doubt.

Define: Identify Design Goals


The results of the research clearly shows why Baskin Robbins app does not meet its expectation. This is because the app does not have a clear focus on user's needs a goals. Taking this into account, I will be identifying the design goals making sure it meets both business and user's need.

  • Consolidate and restructure the sitmap where the user navigating the process is clear and easy to understand.
  • Redesign the layout of the home page where it feels more personalize and fun for the user.
  • Simplify the overall design theme to maintain consistency.

Here is my proposal order of important on the home page based on best practices as well as observation on other fast food chain store app.

  1. Ability to place order of take out ice cream or custom made cake
  2. Ability to find the nearest store location
  3. Ability to see any deals or promotions
  4. Ability to view my account
  5. Ability to pay with gift cards or credit cards on file.
  6. Ability to view existing or recent orer that was placed prior to pickup

Define: Redesign User Flow


Redesign the user flow will have better understanding its hierarchy and organizing the category where it belongs to.

Results


Evaluate: How to determine the result of my redesign

Overall I feel pretty confident that the redesign is better than the existing design. I belive the redesign can help increase in conversion rate, overall user journey and experience. It decrease in user spending time thinking what is the next step he/she should take. It also decrease in amount of time spend to reach's user needs and goals.

What I learned:

  1. Importance of user testing rather making an assumptions. Lack of user data and testing through different stage of the process can lead the product to fail.
  2. I have learned heuristic evaluation can sometime be very challenging to spot problems and finding out what works and what does not work. This is all based on each individual experiences and some people get used to the problem that he/she have trouble caught the problem. By repeating doing the apps multiple time can help spot problem easily.
  3. The value of research user needs and goals early stage of the process definitely helps to make a better product.
  4. Doing different methods of research can help design a better product but again testing out at different stage of the process is also equally important as well.
